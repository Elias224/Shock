Our Vision

As incoming freshman at San Jose State University we want to urge our peers to get involved with a struggling homeless community in the surrounding downtown area near campus. Our goal is to give the homeless the resources they need in order for them to survive in the Silicon Valley as well as encouraging them to better their lives. As active members of the city, it's our duty to give time and resources to those who need it most.
